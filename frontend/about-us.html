GOD & AUDACITY
Our Story

Born During a Crisis.
Built to Last.

Jemena Trading Limited was founded in 2020, a period marked by global upheaval and industrial uncertainty. As essential industries struggled with supply chain disruptions, we emerged as a trusted force in the chemical sector — ensuring consistent, high-quality supply to support industrial continuity.

We established ourselves as a strategic partner for manufacturers, providing cutting-edge chemical formulations, customized supply solutions, and expert consultancy. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and efficiency means every client receives a solution tailored to their production needs.

Over the years we have built strong relationships across manufacturing, mining, water treatment, food processing, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Our global supplier partnerships allow us to offer safe, innovative, and compliant chemical solutions that drive industrial growth across East Africa.
